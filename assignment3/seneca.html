About Seneca College

Welcome to Seneca College

Seneca offers more than an education. With campuses throughout the Greater Toronto Area,
Seneca's size and diversity give students the advantage of partnerships with industry leaders,
the latest in hands-on computer technology, a variety of class sizes, and full-time, part-time
and continuing education options.

History

The life of a college can be measured both by the success
of its graduates and in its physical growth.
Seneca College has graduated thousands of students
since its inception in 1967, and we have
proudly seen them go on to be successful executives, senators,
pilots, and many more.

Safety and Security

Seneca College is committed to providing a safe,
secure learning and working environment for its
students and employees.

Security officers are present on a 24-hour basis at
our King, Markham, Mewnham, and Seneca@York campuses.
Security officers respond to safety and Security threats,
first aid, manage access control, and provide general information
